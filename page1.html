Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to get started with GenieMind?

To get started, first you must turn the Lights ON.
Switch for light can be found below the image of Stanley, which can be found behind the GenieMind. 

How to use Microphone Interaction?

For interaction using microphone, follow the below procedure's.

Step 1: Hold the ArrowDown button to start microphone, then ask your query.
Step 2: Release the ArrowDown button after completing your query.
Step 3: GenieMind will process your query and give's appropriate response.
(Note: This must be followed on each interaction)

How to use text input?

After turning ON the lights, you can find a floating text input bar. Type your query and press ENTER for getting result.

What kind of questions I can ask?

You can ask any kind of questions related to MCA Department and some basic interactive questions.
Please pardon me if any of your questions aren't answered properly, please feel free to Contact Me. :)

What is Augmented Reality?

Augmented reality is an interactive experience of a real-world environment where the objects that reside in the real world are enhanced by computer-generated perceptual information, sometimes across multiple sensory modalities, including visual, auditory, haptic, somatosensory and olfactory.

What is a Virtual Agent?

A virtual agent is a contact center capability that uses a virtual character, created through computer generation, animation and artificial intelligence (AI), to serve customers via chatbot functionality. The intelligent virtual agent can answer customer questions and provide information.
